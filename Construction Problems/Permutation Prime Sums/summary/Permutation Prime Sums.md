# Permutation Prime Sums

**CSES ID:** 3423  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/3423  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Given $n$, produce two permutations $a$ and $b$ of $1,\dots,n$ such that for every position $i$ the sum $a_i+b_i$ is a prime number. Any valid pair of permutations is accepted.

## Input
A single integer $n$.

## Output
Print two permutations on separate lines, or `IMPOSSIBLE` if no such pair exists.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 10^5$
