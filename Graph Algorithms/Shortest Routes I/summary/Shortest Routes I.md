# Shortest Routes I

**CSES ID:** 1671  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1671  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

A directed weighted graph has $n$ cities and $m$ flights with positive edge weights. Compute the shortest path length from city $1$ to every city.

## Input
$n$ and $m$, then $m$ directed edges $a \to b$ with length $c$.

## Output
$n$ integers: the shortest distance from city $1$ to each city.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 10^5$
- $1 \le m \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le a,b \le n$
- $1 \le c \le 10^9$
