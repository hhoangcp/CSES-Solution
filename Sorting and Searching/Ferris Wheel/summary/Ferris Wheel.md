# Ferris Wheel

**CSES ID:** 1090  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1090  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

There are $n$ children with given weights. Each gondola holds at most two children and total weight at most $x$. Minimize the number of gondolas needed.

## Input
First line: integers $n$ and $x$. Next line: $n$ weights $p_1,\ldots,p_n$.

## Output
Print one integer: the minimum number of gondolas needed.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le x \le 10^9$
- $1 \le p_i \le x$
