# Stick Lengths

**CSES ID:** 1074  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1074  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

There are $n$ sticks whose lengths you may extend or shorten, paying cost equal to the absolute change in length. Make all sticks equal at minimum total cost.

## Input
First line: integer $n$. Next line: $n$ integers $p_1,\ldots,p_n$.

## Output
Print one integer: the minimum total cost.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le p_i \le 10^9$
