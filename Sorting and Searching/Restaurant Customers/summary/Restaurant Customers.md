# Restaurant Customers

**CSES ID:** 1619  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1619  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

You are given the arrival and departure times of $n$ customers. Determine the maximum number of customers simultaneously present.

## Input
First line: integer $n$. Then $n$ lines, each with arrival $a$ and departure $b$. All times are distinct.

## Output
Print one integer: the maximum number of customers present at once.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le a < b \le 10^9$
