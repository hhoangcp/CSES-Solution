> Problem summary.

Given the ids of $n$ consecutive songs, find the length of the longest contiguous block in which every song appears at most once.

## Input
First line: integer $n$. Next line: $n$ integers $k_1,\ldots,k_n$.

## Output
Print one integer: the length of the longest block of unique songs.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le k_i \le 10^9$
