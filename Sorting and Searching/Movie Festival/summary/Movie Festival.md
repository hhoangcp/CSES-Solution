# Movie Festival

**CSES ID:** 1629  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1629  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

You know the start and end times of $n$ movies and want to watch as many as possible in full without overlap.

## Input
First line: integer $n$. Then $n$ lines, each with two integers $a$ and $b$ (start and end).

## Output
Print one integer: the maximum number of non-overlapping movies.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le a < b \le 10^9$
