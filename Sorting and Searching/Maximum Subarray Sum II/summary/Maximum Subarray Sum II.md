# Maximum Subarray Sum II

**CSES ID:** 1644  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1644  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Find the maximum sum of a contiguous subarray whose length is between $a$ and $b$.

## Input
First line: integers $n$, $a$, $b$. Second line: $n$ integers $x_1,\ldots,x_n$.

## Output
Print one integer: the maximum subarray sum with length in $[a,b]$.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le a \le b \le n$
- $-10^9 \le x_i \le 10^9$
