# Dice Probability

**CSES ID:** 1725  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1725  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Roll a standard six-sided die $n$ times. Compute the probability that the total sum of the outcomes lies in the inclusive range $[a,b]$. Print it to six decimals.

## Input
A single line with three integers $n$, $a$, $b$.

## Output
Print the probability rounded to six decimal places (round half to even).

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 100$
- $1 \le a \le b \le 6n$
