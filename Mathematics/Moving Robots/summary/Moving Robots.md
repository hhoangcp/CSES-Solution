# Moving Robots

**CSES ID:** 1726  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1726  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Each square of an $8 \times 8$ board initially contains one robot. On each turn every robot independently picks uniformly among its available (in-board) four-direction moves and takes one step (multiple robots may share a square). Compute the expected number of empty squares after $k$ turns.

## Input
A single integer $k$.

## Output
Print the expected number of empty squares rounded to six decimal places (round half to even).

## Constraints
- $1 \le k \le 100$
