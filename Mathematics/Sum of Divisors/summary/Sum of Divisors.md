# Sum of Divisors

**CSES ID:** 1082  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1082  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Let $\sigma(i)$ be the sum of positive divisors of $i$. Compute $\sum_{i=1}^{n} \sigma(i)$ modulo $10^9+7$.

## Input
A single integer $n$.

## Output
Print $\sum_{i=1}^{n} \sigma(i)$ modulo $10^9+7$.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 10^{12}$
