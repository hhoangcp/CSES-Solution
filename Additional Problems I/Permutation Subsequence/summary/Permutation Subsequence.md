# Permutation Subsequence

**CSES ID:** 3404  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/3404  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Both input arrays are permutations. Find their longest common subsequence.

## Input
Integers $n$ and $m$, then permutation $a$ of length $n$, then permutation $b$ of length $m$.

## Output
Print the length of the longest common subsequence, then any such subsequence.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n, m \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $1 \le a_i \le n$
- $1 \le b_i \le m$
