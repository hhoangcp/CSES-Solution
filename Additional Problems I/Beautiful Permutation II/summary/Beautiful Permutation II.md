# Beautiful Permutation II

**CSES ID:** 3175  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/3175  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

A permutation of $1,2,\ldots,n$ is *beautiful* when no two adjacent entries differ by exactly one. Given $n$, produce the lexicographically smallest beautiful permutation, or report that none exists.

## Input
A single integer $n$.

## Output
Print the lexicographically smallest beautiful permutation, or `NO SOLUTION`.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 10^6$
