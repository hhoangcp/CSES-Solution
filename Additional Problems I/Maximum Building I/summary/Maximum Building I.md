# Maximum Building I

**CSES ID:** 1147  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1147  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

A grid of size $n \times m$ consists of empty cells and tree cells. Find the largest area of an axis-aligned rectangle consisting entirely of empty cells (no tree may be inside it).

## Input
Integers $n$ and $m$, then $n$ rows of characters where `.` is empty and `*` is a tree.

## Output
Print the maximum rectangular area of empty cells.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n, m \le 1000$
