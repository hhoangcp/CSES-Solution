# Elevator Rides

**CSES ID:** 1653  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1653  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

There are $n$ people of known weights and a single elevator with weight limit $x$. Determine the fewest elevator rides needed to bring everyone up, grouping people per ride without exceeding the limit.

## Input
Integers $n$ and $x$, then $n$ weights $w_i$.

## Output
The minimum number of rides.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 20$
- $1 \le x \le 10^9$
- $1 \le w_i \le x$
