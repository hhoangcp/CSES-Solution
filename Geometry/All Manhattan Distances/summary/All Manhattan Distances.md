# All Manhattan Distances

**CSES ID:** 3411  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/3411  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

You are given a set of distinct planar points. The goal is to compute the total of the Manhattan distances over every unordered pair of points.

## Input
First an integer $n$, then $n$ lines each with two integers $x$ and $y$ describing a distinct point.

## Output
Print the sum of Manhattan distances over all pairs of points.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $-10^9 \le x, y \le 10^9$
