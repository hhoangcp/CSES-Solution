# Bouncing Ball Steps

**CSES ID:** 3215  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/3215  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

A ball starts at the top-left of an $n \times m$ grid moving diagonally and reflects at borders. After $k$ steps, report the ball's cell and the number of direction changes so far.

## Input
First line: $t$ tests. Then $t$ lines each with $n$, $m$ and $k$.

## Output
For each test, print three integers: the row, column, and number of direction changes.

## Constraints
- $1 \le t \le 1000$
- $2 \le n, m \le 10^9$
- $0 \le k \le 10^{18}$
