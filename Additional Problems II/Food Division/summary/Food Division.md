# Food Division

**CSES ID:** 1189  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/1189  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

$n$ children sit around a round table; each has some food and needs some, with totals matching. In one step a child passes one unit to a neighbor. Find the minimum steps to satisfy everyone.

## Input
First line: $n$. Next line: current amounts $a_i$. Last line: required amounts $b_i$.

## Output
One integer: the minimum number of steps.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $0 \le a_i, b_i \le 10^6$
