# Grid Coloring II

**CSES ID:** 3312  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/3312  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

An $n \times m$ grid is filled with characters `A`, `B`, `C`. Replace every cell with a different one of `A`/`B`/`C` so that no two orthogonally adjacent cells share the same character. Output any valid recoloring or report impossibility.

## Input
First line: $n$ and $m$. Then $n$ lines of $m$ characters each.

## Output
$n$ lines of $m$ characters giving a valid grid, or `IMPOSSIBLE` if none exists.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n, m \le 500$
