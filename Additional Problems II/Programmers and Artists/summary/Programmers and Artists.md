# Programmers and Artists

**CSES ID:** 2426  
**Full statement:** https://cses.fi/problemset/task/2426  
**Time limit:** 1.00s | **Memory limit:** 512MB

> Problem summary.

Hire exactly $a$ programmers and $b$ artists from $n$ applicants; each applicant has a programming skill and an artistic skill and is assigned to one role. Maximize the sum of skills in the chosen roles.

## Input
First line: $a$, $b$ and $n$. Then $n$ lines each with programming skill $x$ and artistic skill $y$.

## Output
One integer: the maximum achievable sum of skills.

## Constraints
- $1 \le n \le 2 \cdot 10^5$
- $0 \le a, b \le n$
- $a+b \le n$
- $1 \le x, y \le 10^9$
